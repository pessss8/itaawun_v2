## 3-Minute Pitch Script

**[0:00–0:30] Hook**
> "Imagine being a student at IIUM, struggling to afford basic needs. You're too proud to ask for charity, but every gig you find on WhatsApp comes with risks. No verification. No accountability. Just hope. This is the reality for 7–8% of our classmates—Asnaf students who deserve better."

**[0:30–1:00] Solution**
> "iTa'awun is a centralized platform where IIUM students can safely offer and book services. But unlike WhatsApp groups, every user is verified. Vendors are approved by admin. CGPA is monitored to protect academics. And B40 students get priority access. It's not just a marketplace—it's a welfare-first ecosystem."

**[1:00–1:45] Innovation**
> "What makes us different? Three things: First, we're built on Maqasid al-Shariah—Islamic principles of justice, dignity, and cooperation. Second, we have admin oversight—universities can monitor, intervene, and protect students. Third, we generate impact reports for Zakat authorities—making it easy to prove outcomes and secure ongoing support."

**[1:45–2:15] Traction**
> "We've built the full platform—login, browsing, bookings, ratings, admin dashboard. It's live right now at pessss8.github.io/itaawun. In our pilot semester, we project 100+ B40 students earning RM200–400 monthly. That's RM20,000+ in direct income support—without charity stigma."

**[2:15–2:45] Ask**
> "We're seeking IPO grant support to scale campus-wide, market to students, and develop premium features. With your backing, we can expand to all IIUM faculties and eventually license to other Islamic universities."

**[2:45–3:00] Close**
> "iTa'awun isn't just a gig platform. It's dignity. It's safety. It's cooperation in action. Scan the QR code to try the demo. Thank you."
